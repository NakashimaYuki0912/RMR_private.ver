About This Version

This mod is an unofficial derivative maintenance version based on RogueLike Mod REBORN. I greatly enjoyed the original mod and wanted to experience more content beyond where its development ended, so I began maintaining and expanding it after official updates ceased.

Every journey begins at Urban Myth. Players must choose between Normal Battles, Elite Battles, Boss Battles, Shops, Rest Sites, and Mysterious Events while gradually building their team and advancing toward the Impuritas Civitatis endgame.

This page contains spoilers for the later chapters and ending content of Library of Ruina.

This version does not represent the original creator, the REBORN team, or Project Moon.

Major Changes

  • Redesigned RMR Entry and Start Menu

    The RMR icon in the upper-right corner of the Reception screen now opens a full-screen start menu.

    From this menu, you can start a new run, continue your current journey, challenge Floor Realizations, view the gameplay guide, access the Permanent Compendium, reset permanent progression, or exit.

  • Added Floor Realization Challenges

    You can now independently challenge the final Realization battle of each of the ten original Floors.

    Clearing a Floor Realization for the first time permanently unlocks that Floor's exclusive Abnormality Pages and E.G.O., allowing them to appear in future reward pools and Shop selections.

    Previously cleared Realizations can be challenged again, but their first-clear rewards cannot be obtained more than once.

  • Reworked Abnormality Page Acquisition

    Most regular Abnormality Pages are now obtained through random post-battle rewards, Shops, special events, and other sources.

    Abnormality Pages and E.G.O. exclusive to a Floor Realization will only be added to future selection pools after that Floor has been cleared for the first time.

  • Added a Permanent Compendium

    The Permanent Compendium records all unlocked Key Pages, Combat Pages, Abnormality Pages, and E.G.O.

    You can also use it to inspect their attributes, abilities, and upgraded effects.

    When challenging a Floor Realization, you can use content recorded in the Permanent Compendium to assemble a temporary team.

    After the challenge ends, your original run configuration will be restored. The temporary setup will not overwrite or interfere with your normal run save.

    The Permanent Compendium is not the starting inventory for a new run. After beginning a new journey, you must still collect resources and build your team again from scratch.

  • Expanded the Impuritas Civitatis Chapter

    The standard route now extends into Impuritas Civitatis and includes several Impuritas Civitatis-level Receptions.

    Possible final bosses include The Black Silence and the Distorted Reverberation Ensemble. Each run will feature one of them as its final encounter.

    Defeating each boss for the first time will also permanently unlock special content, providing additional rewards for future runs.

  • Added Abnormality Battle Nodes

    Starting from Chapter 3, Abnormality Battles may appear along the route.

    These encounters function as high-risk, high-reward Elite Battles and provide additional opportunities to obtain Abnormality Pages.

    Abnormality Battle nodes will not appear during Chapters 1 or 2. Any such positions will instead be replaced with Rest Sites.

Before Subscribing

  • This version uses the same internal mod ID as the original RMR. Do not enable both versions at the same time. It is recommended that you keep only one of them installed or enabled.

  • The Continue option is located in the RMR start menu. It will only appear when a valid run save exists.

  • Starting a new run will overwrite your current run save. Permanent Compendium progress, Floor Realization clear records, and first-clear records for special bosses are stored separately and will be preserved.

  • Before switching between versions or installing a major update, it is recommended that you back up the LogueSave save directory.

  • Known major mod conflict: Sayo to Everyone.

  • Simplified Chinese is currently the primary language used for development, maintenance, and testing. English and Korean text are included, but their completeness and accuracy still require further review.

  • After updating the mod, fully close and restart the game before playing.

Bug Reports

This version is currently developed and maintained by one person with AI assistance. Testing coverage is limited, so issues involving progression, UI display, localization, or mod compatibility may still occur.

If you encounter a problem, please report it in the comments section of this page.

When reporting an issue, please include as much of the following information as possible:

  • Your game language
  • Your enabled mod list
  • The current chapter and node
  • Steps required to reproduce the issue
  • Relevant screenshots
  • The Build information and exception messages from Player.log

Providing this information will make it much easier to identify and resolve the problem.
